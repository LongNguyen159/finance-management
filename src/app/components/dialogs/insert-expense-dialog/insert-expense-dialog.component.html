{{ option }} Amount

Modify the amount of your existing entries. If your expenses, e.g., Shopping, Groceries, change frequently, you can insert them here. Examples:

  • Single Value: 20 or -30
  • Multiple Values: 20 30 -10 (separated by spaces)
  • This also works: 20 + 30 - 10

Letters and special characters are not allowed.
