User Manual for Financial Visualisation App

Simplifying Finance Management for Everyone

About the App

This app makes it easy to visualise your financial data without needing Excel or technical knowledge. With just a few simple inputs, you can manage your finances better!

Getting Started

To begin, look for the "+" button at the top right corner. Clicking this will open a menu where you can enter your financial information.

Input Fields Explained

Hereâ€™s a quick guide to help you fill in the input fields:

  • Type: Choose what you're entering:
    • Income: Money you earn (no need to fill out the "Source from" field).
    • Expense: Money you spend. Use "Source from" to specify where the expense comes from (e.g., rent or groceries). This is optional. If not specified, it is automatically understood that expense has source from income.
    • Tax: The tax amount. You can only enter this once!
  • Name: Give your entry a name (like "Rent" or "Groceries"). This helps you remember what the money is for.
  • Value: Enter the amount of money (like "800" for rent).
  • Source from (optional): This is mostly for you to specify what made up an expense. For example, if you enter "Housing," you might want to specify it includes "Rent" and "Utilities.", which has source from "Housing".

What Happens Next?

As you add entries:

  • If you enter more than one income, the app will calculate your total income.
  • If you enter a tax amount, the app will show your "usable income" after tax is deducted.

Note: You should not enter many entries with the same name, although it might work, but the total expense calculation will not be accurate anymore.

Notes

Your data stays local! Everything runs right on your device, so there's no need to worry about privacy. Your information is safe with you.
