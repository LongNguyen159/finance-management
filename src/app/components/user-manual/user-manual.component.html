User Manual for Easy Sankey

Easily Visualise Your Financial Data

About Easy Sankey

Easy Sankey helps you create clear and simple financial visualisations without the need for Excel or technical skills. Just a few easy inputs, and you can see where your money goes!

Getting Started

To get started, click the "+ Add Input" button in the top-right corner (Of the chart page, not this page of course!). This will open a menu where you can enter your financial information.

Input Fields Explained

Here’s a quick overview to help you fill in the input fields:

  • Type: Choose the type of entry:
    • Income: Your earnings (no need to fill out the "Part of" field).
    • Expense: Your spending. Use "Part of" to specify what the expense is tied to (e.g., rent or groceries). This is optional. If not specified, the expense will be assumed to come from income.
    • Tax: Your tax amount. You can only enter this once.
  • Name: Give your entry a name (e.g., "Rent" or "Groceries") to easily track it.
  • Amount: Enter the amount of money (e.g., "800" for rent).
  • Part of (optional): This field lets you break down expenses.
    • Example: For "Housing," you can specify that it includes "Rent" and "Utilities," both of which are part of "Housing."
    • Default behavior: If no "Part of" is specified for an expense, it will automatically be linked to your income.
    • "Default income" option: Choose this option if you want the expense to link from your assumed default income.

What Happens Next?

As you add entries:

  • If you enter more than one income, Easy Sankey will calculate your total income.
  • If you enter a tax amount, the app will show your "usable income" after taxes are deducted.

Note: Avoid entering multiple entries with the same name to ensure accurate expense calculations.

Notes from Developer

This app runs locally on your device, so your data stays safe with you! No need to worry about privacy issues. Don’t believe me? Try turning off your internet connection and see if the app still works!

(Heck, I don’t even have a server)

For the technical folks, this app uses LocalStorage to save your data.


Want to start fresh? You can clear all your data by clicking the button at the bottom of the page. This will reset the app like it’s the first time you’ve installed it, and you’ll see the welcome page again if you restart the app. Please note that all your saved data will be lost.
