User Manual for Easy Sankey

Easily Visualise Your Financial Data

About Easy Sankey

Easy Sankey helps you create clear and simple financial visualisations without the need for Excel or technical skills. Just a few easy inputs, and you can see where your money goes!

Getting Started

To get started, click the "+ Add Input" button in the top-right corner (Of the chart page, not this page of course!). This will open a menu where you can enter your financial information.

Input Fields Explained

Here's a quick overview to help you fill in the input fields:

  • Type: Choose the type of entry:
    • Income: Your earnings (no need to fill out the "Source from" field).
    • Expense: Your spending. Use "Source from" to specify what the expense is tied to (e.g., rent or groceries). This is optional. If not specified, the expense will be assumed to come from income.
    • Tax: Your tax amount. You can only enter this once.
  • Name: Give your entry a name (e.g., "Rent" or "Groceries") to easily track it.
  • Value: Enter the amount of money (e.g., "800" for rent).
  • Source from (optional): This field lets you break down expenses. For example, for "Housing," you can specify that it includes "Rent" and "Utilities," both of which source from "Housing."

What Happens Next?

As you add entries:

  • If you enter more than one income, Easy Sankey will calculate your total income.
  • If you enter a tax amount, the app will show your "usable income" after taxes are deducted.

Note: Avoid entering multiple entries with the same name to ensure accurate expense calculations.

Notes

Your data stays on your device! Everything is processed locally, so you don't need to worry about privacy. Your information is completely safe.

(And no, there isn't even a server involved!)
