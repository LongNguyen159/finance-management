User Manual for Easy Sankey

Easily Visualise Your Financial Data

Why Easy Sankey?

Easy Sankey helps you create clear and simple financial visualisations without the need for Excel or technical skills. Just a few easy inputs, and you can see where your money goes!

Getting Started

On the main page, click the "Edit Input" button in the top-right corner. This will open a menu where you can enter your financial information.

Input Fields Explained

Hereâ€™s a quick overview to help you fill in the input fields:

  • Type: Choose the type of entry:
    • Income: Your earnings.
    • Expense: Your spending.
    • Tax: Your tax amount. You can only enter this once.
  • Name: Give your entry a name (e.g., "Rent" or "Pet food").
  • Amount: Enter the amount of money (e.g., "800" for rent).
  • Category (optional): Assign a category to your entry. This can be useful for reportings and budget setting for each category.
